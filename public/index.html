What We Do?

We provide an online platform for current restaurant, allowing them to create a dashboard for their own restaurant and create info (like dish menu) for their restaurant.

User can upload their comments about a specific restaurant like what they did in Yelp. With this function user can find the best rated restaurants nearby.

User can reserve the restaurants beforehand through this online platform so that they don't have to wait from the long line.
